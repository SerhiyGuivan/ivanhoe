Frequently asked questions

Can I trial Ivanhoe for free?
You can try our plans 30 days free trial. No credit card is required.
How secure is Ivanhoe?
We take security of your information seriously. Your data is stored in a secure third-party database provided by Digital Ocean in encrypted format and sent and received over a secure SSL connection.
Can I download Ivanhoe?
No. Ivanhoeis a fully-hosted service. We think this is a better solution as it means we can continue to offer you updates and new features as we go without requiring you to download anything. You can access your account safely anywhere there is an internet connection.
How does your pricing work?
Our pricing is tiered based on the number of conversions you make at the networks. People set to not appear in the schedule or that are archived are free. Websites, Networks and Intergrations are all unlimited.
What types of payment do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, Paypal and iDeal. Fees are debited from your account automatically each billing period and we email you a receipt.
How long are your contracts?
Our contracts are monthly. You can upgrade, downgrade or cancel your account at any time. You won’t be charged any further once you’ve cancelled your account.
